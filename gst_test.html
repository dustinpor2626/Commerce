We are committed to make taxation simple for small business owners in India. Since GST is set to replace various indirect taxes.

GST Registration & Procedure


  • Every taxpayer should get registered within 30 days of becoming liable for registration however for casual tax payer and non-resident they must get registered within 5 days prior to the commencement of business.

  • Taxpayers having single PAN have business in different sate have to take registration each state, however if any person have vertical business in same state then he may opt for different registration.

  • The threshold limit for the taxability is whose turnover exceeds Rs.20 lakh and Rs.10 Lakhs for north east state.

  • These are the list of compulsory registration:

    • Persons making any inter-state taxable supply
    • Input service distributor
    • Casual taxable persons
    • Person who supply goods or services other than branded, services, through electronic commerce operator
    • person who are required to pay tax on reverse charge basis
    • Every electronic commerce operator
    • Non-resident taxable persons
    • An aggregator who supplies services under his brand name of his trade name
    • Person who are required to deduct tax under sec 37
    • Persons who supply or services on behalf of other registered taxable person whether as an agent or otherwise
    • Such other person or class of persons as may be notified by the central Govt. or a state Govt. on the recommendation of the council.
